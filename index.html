PYRAMIDS

What Are Pyramids??
Pyramids are ancient stone structures built thousands of years ago, mainly in Egypt. They were designed to honor pharaohs and important leaders
Purpose of Pyramids The main purpose of pyramids was to serve as tombs for pharaohs, protecting their bodies and treasures for the afterlife. Famous Pyramids Great Pyramid of Giza: The most famous and largest pyramid. Pyramid of Khafre: Known for its impressive height and smooth casing stones. Pyramid of Menkaure: Smaller but still an important monument. How They Were Built Pyramids were built with huge stones stacked carefully using advanced techniques. Their triangular shape helped them last thousands of years. Why Pyramids Are Important Pyramids are symbols of history, culture, and mystery. They attract tourists and historians worldwide and show the amazing skills of ancient civilizations.
